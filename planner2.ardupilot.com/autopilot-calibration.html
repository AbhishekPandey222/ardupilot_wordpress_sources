After installing firmware, follow these steps to calibrate and configure your autopilot to work with your vehicle.

1 Prerequisites

Before preforming setup, ensure that:
  • The vehicle is completely built to flight condition.
  • Propellers are removed.
  • Firmware has been loaded onto the autopilot.
  • Autopilot is connected to APM Planner.

2 Select Initial Setup, Mandatory Setup

Select Initial Setup and Mandatory Setup to view the menu of calibration wizards.

3 Complete the Calibration Wizards

Perform each of the calibration wizards shown below
  • Select frame type (copter only)
  • Accelerometer calibration
  • Compass calibration
  • Radio calibration
  • Failsafe calibration
  • 3DR radio setup
