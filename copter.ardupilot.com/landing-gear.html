Copter supports retractable Landing Gear activated by a servo mechanism (like these sold by Hobby King). The gear can be manually retracted/deployed with an auxiliary switch and they can also be activated automatically (retract after take-off, deploy during landing). [note]Support for landing gear was introduced in AC 3.3[/note]

Connecting to the Pixhawk

Most retractable landing gear will require just a single PWM cable to connected to one of the AUX outputs on the back of the Pixhawk (AUX1 ~ AUX4).   [note]The signal wire (usually white) should be connected to the lower most "S" pin.[/note]

Setup through the Mission Planner

Use the Mission Planner's Full Parameter List (or equivalent) to set the following parameters:
  • RCn_FUNCTION to 29 "Landing Gear", where n is the number for the connected servo (e.g. RC9_FUNCTION for AUX1, RC10_FUNCTION for AUX2 etc.)
  • LGR_SERVO_DEPLOY — servo PWM value required to deploy the landing gear.
  • LGR_SERVO_RTRACT — servo PWM value required to retract the landing gear.
[caption id="attachment_14434" align="alignnone" width="1024"] Mission Planner: Landing Gear Configuration[/caption] Set Ch7 or Ch8 Opt to "Landing Gear" in order to enable manual control.  When the switch is in the "low" position the gear will be deployed, "high" will be retracted, "middle" will be in auto retract/deploy meaning the gear will retract after take-off and deploy before landing.
