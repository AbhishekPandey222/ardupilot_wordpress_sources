This topic lists Rover's control modes. Autopilot control modes are controlled through a radio transmitter switch, via mission commands, or using commands from a ground station (GCS) or companion computer. The control modes supported for Rover are: [pagelist_navmenu] For a 3 position switch initially set 5 and 6 to MANUAL, 3 and 4 to LEARNING and 1 and 2 to AUTO.
