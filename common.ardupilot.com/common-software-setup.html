APM open-source, community-supported software is an important component to the APM autopilot system. In addition to the APM autopilot hardware, Mission Planner software allows you to turn any RC plane, copter, or rover into an APM autonomous vehicle. Download Mission Planner onto your ground station computer to:
  • Easily program your autopilot with the latest code for your type of vehicle.
  • Configure your RC control options and flight modes.
  • Plan missions for your autonomous vehicle to execute, including waypoints and events.
  • Load your missions to your autopilot before flying.
  • Experiment with different settings in the HIL flight simulator.
