Connect to Mission Planner
  • Start Mission Planner and hook up your APM or PX4 board via the USB port.
  • Ensure that the correct COM port for your APM or PX4 is selected and click on the "Connect" button on the right.
  • With MAVlink connected, move the APM board and you should see the "Artificial Horizon" in the HUD move accordingly.
  • In an artificial horizon, when the aircraft tilts to the right, the horizon tilts to the left. (Just tilt your head and you can visualize this). This is normal! Please don't tell us it's reversed ;-)
  • In the below example, the quad is tilted a bit to the right.
  • Unless you have the GPS module connected, are outside and have GPS lock, the map will not show your current position.
